Over half the world's population lives under the threat of Malaria

Each year, hundreds of thousands die from this deadly, yet preventable illness.

Malaria is a vector-borne illness spread by the bite of a mosquito. whose saliva allows a parasite of the genus Plasmodium to enter the bloodstream.

Initial symptoms will appear 7-15 days after infection, and may include:
  • fever
  • headache
  • chills
  • vomiting
